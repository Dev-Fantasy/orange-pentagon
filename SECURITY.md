# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of our projects seriously. If you find any security vulnerabilities or bugs that could compromise the safety of this project, please do not report them via public issues.

Instead, please report them directly to us by sending an email to:
**devfantasy.void@gmail.com**

### What to include in your report?
To help us understand and fix the issue faster, please include:
- A brief description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- Potential impact of the bug.

We will acknowledge your email as soon as possible and work on a fix. Thank you for helping us keep our project secure!
